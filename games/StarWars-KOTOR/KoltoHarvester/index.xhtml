Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic — Kolto Harvester

By William Swartzendruber

Perform these steps when attempting to solve the kolto harvester puzzle:

  1. Fill container.
  2. Transfer from container to injector.
  3. Dump container.
  4. Transfer from injector to container.
  5. Fill injector.
  6. Transfer from injector to container.
  7. Dump container.
  8. Transfer from injector to container.
  9. Fill injector.
  10. Transfer from injector to container.
