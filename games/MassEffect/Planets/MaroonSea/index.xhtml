Mass Effect — Planets — Maroon Sea

Introduction

This page contains maps of the open world in Mass Effect’s Maroon Sea cluster, with three maps.

  • Initial maps are simply screenshots of the in-game map directly after landing.
  • Details maps call things out that are not necessarily obvious while playing the game:
    • All anomalies and minerals are presented upfront.
    • Other items of interest not otherwise marked are called out using a yellow X, whether they be loot (inventory) or enemies (XP).
  • Completed maps are simply screenshots of the in-game map when everything there has been finished. Remember, though, that not everything is reflected here which is why the Details maps have been created.
