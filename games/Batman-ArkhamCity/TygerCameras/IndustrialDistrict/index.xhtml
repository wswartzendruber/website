Batman: Arkham City — Tyger Cameras — Industrial District

Introduction

This page contains the locations of all Tyger cameras in Arkham City’s Industrial District. Each camera has two screenshots:

  • The in-game map of Batman standing in front of that camera,
  • Batman locked onto that camera from that location on the map,

Because Batman is always shown here nearby a camera, Batman’s location on the map is also each camera’s approximate location.
